Orange Chicken

Description

Orange Chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish. It is known for its vibrant orange glaze and is often served over rice or noodles.

This orange chicken recipe is sticky, sweet, and delicious. It's easier to make than you think for delicious takout at home.

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 2 tablespoons water

Chicken:

  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine ingredients for sauce into a saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil
  3. Remove the sauce from heat, and cool for 10 to 15 minutes
  4. Mix chicken with 1 cup cooled sauce in a bowl; seal it in the fridge for at least 2 hours
  5. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  6. Remove chicken from the fridge and coat it with seasoned flour
  7. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  8. Cook chicken and sauce in the hop skillet until cooked through
  9. Serve over rice and enjoy!
